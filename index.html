Bellarmine Computer Science Club is dedicated to working closely with the computer science department to create a collaborative, learning-oriented environment. We invite students whho excel in computer science classes to join our expanding team of programmers.

Our mission is to create projects each school year that benefit Bellarmine and/or the greater Tacoma community. We want students to explore the synergy of creativity and programming to manufacture influential contributions.

The Bellarmine Computer Science Club is invested in representing women in STEM. We pride ourselves on the majority of our members being female and cultivating an accepting atmosphere and culture.
